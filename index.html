Estate Description — For Bid Preparation
▾
This section is provided for your reference only. Do not edit — use this information to inform your proposal in the sections that follow.
Estate Name: Golden Gate Estate
Location: South B, Nairobi, Kenya (approximately 3–5 km south-east of Nairobi CBD)
Type: Gated residential estate comprising maisonettes arranged in separate courts
Phase 1 Scope (this bid): 4 residential courts, the main gate, shared estate roads, and common areas
Approximate Unit Count (Phase 1): To be confirmed at site visit stage

Access & Layout:
— One main vehicle and pedestrian gate serving as the primary entry/exit point
— Multiple feeder streets adjoin the estate perimeter, creating secondary access challenges
— Shared internal estate roads connect the courts
— Communal parking areas, open green spaces, and a field within the estate
— A school is located adjacent to the estate; pupils have been observed using the estate as a shortcut

Known Security Challenges:
— No estate-wide CCTV coverage (one court has its own CCTV; others do not)
— Inconsistent perimeter and road lighting across courts
— No formal visitor management system or visitor register at the main gate
— No resident vehicle identification scheme (stickers or tags); resident and non-resident vehicles are indistinguishable
— No formal policy governing contractors, domestic workers, or service provider access
— Boda boda and commercial vehicle congestion at and around the main gate
— Unauthorised persons and vehicles passing through the estate unchallenged
— A visitor curfew (2200 hrs) exists but is not consistently enforced
— No existing radio communication infrastructure for security coordination

Note: Specific court names, internal layouts, and resident numbers are withheld at this stage. Shortlisted bidders will receive a detailed site brief and guided walk-through before final proposals are requested.
